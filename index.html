Features available with Project Jellyfish

Project Jellyfish is an open-source cloud broker platform,
allowing enterprises to effectively manage their cloud resources.

Modular

The Core functionality of Project Jellyfish is easily expanded and customized to your specific needs by creating your own Ruby Gems.

Multi cloud management

Users can very easily purchase cloud services from multiple providers, and monitor services already in use, in case more resources are needed..

Open Source

As a community-driven open source project, we're always on the lookout for new contributors. Contributing modules to our increasing number of services is easy with our step-by-step documentation.

Project and Financial Management

Project Jellyfish allows users to monitor and manage a variety of projects. Managers can easily enforce use quotas, provision resources and track budgets.

Responsive ready

Our story

Like any triumphant tale, Project Jellyfish was born from the notion that cloud service management could and should be better, easier and maybe even enjoyable. In our case, we decidedly chose to focus on the managing of multiple cloud services from a single environment. Early on it was decided that the focus should be on the organization. A clean front-end design means designing an intuitive interface that we refer to as the "Self-Service UI". This interface is the way in which users, managers and administrators can create projects, go through the approval process for budgets and service requirements, and continue to manage the services after they've been provisioned.

Keeping scalability in mind, Project Jellyfish has also focused on a modular back-end. Utilizing a Ruby on Rails engine from which a multitude of modules can be created, our system behind-the-scenes is what allows for the provisioning of complex cloud services with the click of a button.

Now, as a complete open-source cloud broker platform driven by a growing community of developers, designers, and end users, we allow enterprises to manage their cloud resources in a fashion that is enjoyable and super easy for all parties involved.
